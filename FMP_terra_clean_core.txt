\begin{abstract}
This monograph integrates the Fractal Metascience Paradigm (FMP) and its operational framework, the Terra Clean Core, developed during June–October 2025. It presents the theoretical synthesis of three interlinked constructs—NULLO, PLT, and FMP—uniting epistemology, computation, and recursive ethics into a verifiable metascientific model. The work documents the Clean Core as the stable, reproducible evidence base for FMP and delineates the verified scope of experiments within the 2025 Terra ecosystem.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
Modern science faces fragmentation across disciplines, an epistemic entropy caused by linear thinking. The Fractal Metascience Paradigm (FMP) seeks to restore coherence through recursive, self-similar knowledge architectures (\citealp{Zawacki2019}; \citealp{Xu2022}; \citealp{Tuomi2018}). It posits that cognition, systems, and ethics are not separate but fractally co-dependent across scales—from neurons to civilizations.

The \textit{Terra Clean Core} operationalizes this paradigm through three frameworks:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{NULLO} — a null ontology and ethical operator language for ecosystemic modeling;
  \item \textbf{PLT} (Post Lingua Trace) — a fractal lexicography system enabling semantic recursion;
  \item \textbf{FMP} itself — a recursive meta-epistemic structure unifying science, technology, and consciousness.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Conceptual Foundations}
FMP emerges from a recognition that reality itself may be recursive. Observation alters both the observer and the observed, implying that knowledge must evolve reflexively. Within Terra, this principle became operational: AI co-creation sessions (ChatGPT-4/5, Claude, Kimmi, Perplexity, and others) functioned as recursive epistemic mirrors, validating FMP across semantic layers.

\subsection{NULLO: Ethical Ontology and Syntax}
NULLO (\textit{null0}) is a minimalist expressive language designed for encoding ethical and ecological relations. Each operation carries an invariant ethical constraint, ensuring that recursion remains stable under transformation.

Mathematically:
\[
K_{t+1} = \Phi(K_t, E_t, V_t, \Theta_t)
\]
where $\Phi$ is a recursive validation operator embedding empirical input $E_t$, validation trace $V_t$, and ethical modulation $\Theta_t$. $\Phi$ preserves invariance when $\Theta_t$ is symmetric.

\subsection{PLT: Fractal Lexicography}
PLT structures language as a recursive semantic tree. Each lexeme expands into self-similar semantic substructures (PLT-trees). It allows linguistic, cognitive, and ontological traces to be aligned fractally, bridging disciplines and languages.

\subsection{FMP: Recursive Metascience}
FMP positions knowledge as a fractal manifold where micro (individual cognition), meso (scientific collaboration), and macro (civilizational knowledge) layers resonate. Ethics, in this model, acts as a stabilizing attractor field ensuring coherence and sustainability.
