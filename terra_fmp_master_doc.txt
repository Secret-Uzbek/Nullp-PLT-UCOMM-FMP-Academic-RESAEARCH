This omnibus presents the complete theoretical and practical framework of the Fractal Metascience Paradigm (FMP), developed between June and October 2025. The work synthesizes three foundational concepts:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Nullo} --- the ontological zero-point as a quantum-semantic attractor

The paradigm is validated through the \textbf{AIUZ Terra Ecosystem}, a DAO-governed platform implementing these principles in education, ecological monitoring, logistics, and global coordination.

\section{Philosophical Foundations}

\subsection{What is Nullo?}

Nullo ($\emptyset_{\phi}$) represents the ontological zero-point---a state of pure potentiality before any distinction emerges. Unlike mathematical zero or Buddhist emptiness, Nullo is:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Productive} --- generates all possible states
    \item \textbf{Recursive} --- contains itself at all scales
    \item \textbf{Observable} --- manifests through fractal patterns
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Mathematical Formulation}

Let $\mathcal{M}$ be the manifold of all possible knowledge states. Nullo is the attractor:

\begin{equation}
\emptyset_{\phi} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \phi^{-n} \sum_{i=0}^{n} \phi^i S_i
\end{equation}

where $\phi = \frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}$ (golden ratio) and $S_i$ are knowledge states at scale $i$.

\section{Quantum-Semantic Interpretation}

Nullo exhibits quantum-like properties:

\begin{theorem}[Nullo Superposition]
A system in Nullo state exists in superposition of all possible meanings until observed through a particular context.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
By definition, $\emptyset_{\phi}$ contains no fixed interpretation. Upon measurement (contextual collapse), it assumes a specific meaning while maintaining potential for all others. $\square$
\end{proof}

FMP organizes reality into seven fractal levels (L0--L7):

\begin{description}
    \item[L0] Quantum field (uncertainty, superposition, entanglement)
    \item[L1] Atomic-molecular (chemical bonds, molecular recognition)
    \item[L2] Biological (natural selection, symbiosis, adaptation)
    \item[L3] Consciousness (emergence, cultural evolution)
    \item[L4] Technological (feedback, self-organization)
    \item[L5] Planetary (Gaia hypothesis, cycles)
    \item[L6] Cosmic (cosmic evolution, anthropic principle)
    \item[L7] Transcendent (unity, absolute)
\end{description}

Each level contains a holographic projection of all others.

\begin{equation}
